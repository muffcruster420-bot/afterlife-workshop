# Safety — Read Before Listening

- Do not listen while driving, operating machinery, or walking in traffic
- Stop if you feel dizzy, anxious, or uncomfortable
- This is research only — not a medical treatment, not hospice care, not a replacement for a doctor
- Do not use with implanted neurostimulators without clinician approval
- If you have epilepsy, PTSD, or are in active grief crisis, talk to your clinician first
- We do not do readings, we do not contact the dead, we do not charge money
