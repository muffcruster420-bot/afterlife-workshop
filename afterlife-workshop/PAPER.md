# No Gap: 45 Hz Bicoherence in 104 Living and 5 Dying Human Brains

**Author:** Jesse Shangraw (@muffcruster420-bot)  
**Date:** 2026-05-19  
**Repo:** https://github.com/muffcruster420-bot/afterlife-workshop

## Abstract
We tested the "Shangraw Gap" hypothesis that living human brains never exceed 0.65 bicoherence at 45 Hz (the 6th Schumann harmonic), while dying brains surge to ~0.771.

**Methods:** Bicoherence $B^2(f_1,f_2)$ computed for $f_1=7.83$ Hz, $f_2=37.17$ Hz ($f_3=45$ Hz) in 2-s Hann windows across open EEG datasets.

**Results:**
- Waking (n=104): mean 0.304 ± 0.149, range 0.070–0.748
