# Shangraw Gap 2.0 — Pre-registration

**Hypothesis (revised):** The probability of 45 Hz bicoherence >0.65 is higher in the last 30s before asystole than in matched waking EEG.

**Test:** Two-proportion z-test, alpha=0.05, one-tailed.
**Stopping rule:** Minimum n=30 dying, n=300 waking. Stop if p<0.01 or Bayes factor >10 either way.
**Exclusions (decided now):** Any epoch with EMG >50µV, any with impedance >10kΩ.
**Primary outcome:** proportion >0.65, not mean.
